## Part 1 — Academic Mathematical Papers (Tier A)

### 1.1 "Minimal varieties in Riemannian manifolds" (1968)
- James Simons (sole). *Annals of Mathematics*, Vol. 88 No. 1, 62–105.
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.2307/1970556
- Key: "Simons' identity" — elliptic PDE for 2nd fundamental form. Instability of cones for n ≤ 6; Bernstein through dim 8.
- **Cognitive DNA**: reduce phenomenon to canonical equation, let equation do the work. Same pattern later in trading.

### 1.2 "Characteristic forms and geometric invariants" (Chern–Simons, 1974)
- Chern & Simons. *Annals*, Vol. 99 No. 1, 48–69.
- Origin: grew from attacking combinatorial formula for first Pontrjagin number of a 4-manifold; a boundary term resisted analysis, so they studied the boundary term itself.
- Foundational to: Chern–Simons theory in physics (Witten), TQFT, 3-manifold invariants, condensed matter.
- **DNA**: when direct attack fails, the obstruction itself is the discovery.

### 1.3 Context
- PhD Berkeley 1961 under Bertram Kostant (transitivity of holonomy systems).
- Cheeger–Simons differential characters (LNM 1167, 1985).

- Canonical autobiographical talk. Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tj1NyJHLvWA

  - "Secret sauce": (1) great scientists, (2) great infrastructure, (3) open atmosphere — no compartmentalization, weekly research meetings, compensation tied to overall profits.

## Part 6 — Contradictions & tensions

1. **"Never override" vs. rare exceptions** — near-absolute rule with conscious, infrequent exceptions.
2. **"Patterns without reasons" (markets) vs. "math is the language of nature" (science)** — deliberate domain split: adversarial environments destabilize causes; physical law stabilizes them.
3. **Renaissance secrecy vs. Simons Foundation open science** — Simons framed foundation as "give back" counterweight.
4. **"Let smart people do their thing" vs. "never override the model"** — humans *build and improve* the model; once in production the model has authority. Hierarchy is meta-level (research), not decision-level (execution).
5. **Mathematician demanding proof vs. trader demanding only persistence** — different epistemic standards for different games, held in the same mind.
