## 一、核心著作

### 1.1 《What It Takes: Lessons in the Pursuit of Excellence》(2019)

- 出版：Avid Reader Press / Simon & Schuster, 2019-09-17；384 页；ISBN 978-1501158148
- 性质：半自传 + 方法论手册
- 一手来源官网：https://readwhatittakes.com/ ★★★
- 25 Rules 官方列表：https://readwhatittakes.com/25-rules/ ★★★

**25 条"人生与工作法则"完整版（官方站点原文节选，★★★）：**

- Rule #1 **Think big** — "It's as easy to do something big as it is to do something small, so reach for a fantasy worthy of your pursuit, with rewards commensurate to your effort."
- Rule #2 **Executives are made, not born**（高管是后天培养，不是天生）
- Rule #3 **Reach out to people you admire**（主动联系你仰慕的人求建议）
- Rule #4 **Focus on others' problems**（专注别人的问题——"没人比自己的问题更让自己感兴趣"）
- Rule #6 **Information is the most important asset in business**（信息是商业最重要资产；信息越多 → pattern / anomaly 识别越早）
- Rule #7 **Take jobs with steep learning curves**, not just prestigious ones
- Rule #9 **An army of smart people talking openly** solves problems better than any individual
- Rule #12 **Integrity must be unquestionable**（正直不可打折）
- Rule #13 **Be bold** — act when others are frozen
- Rule #16 **Pursue huge opportunities others ignore** — 更难的问题 = 更少竞争 = 更大回报
- Rule #18 **"Time wounds all deals"** — "Often the longer you wait, the more surprises await you."
- Rule #19 **"Don't lose money!!!"** — 黑石内部最高戒律
- Rule #22 **Failure is the best teacher** — analyze openly
- Rule #23 **Hire 10s whenever you can** — "You can always build something around a 10."
- Rule #24 **Stand by good people** in their tough times
- Rule #25 **Help others achieve their dreams**

书中关键章节（据 Admired Leadership 书评 ★）：
- "There Are No Brave, Old People in Finance"（pp. 150-153）— Edgcomb 惨败后建立的投委会法则

- "Pursue Worthy Fantasies" — Think big 的方法论

## 二、反复出现≥3 次的核心论点（真信念）

### 2.1 "It's as easy / hard to do something big as to do something small"（做大事与小事一样难/易）

出现在：
- What It Takes 全书主题 / 官网 Rule #1 ★★★
  https://readwhatittakes.com/25-rules/
- strategy+business "Blackstone's Builder" (Summer 2020, PwC) ★★★
  https://www.strategy-business.com/article/Blackstones-builder
  （访谈人 Daniel Gross + Neil Dhar，2020 年 1 月采访）
- 用于解释他 MIT 捐 3.5 亿美元、办 Schwarzman Scholars 清华项目的动机

**书中原话（两种措辞并存，★★★）：**
> "If you're going to commit yourself to something, it's as easy to do something big as it is to do something small. Both will consume your time and energy, so make sure your fantasy is worthy of your pursuit."

> "But I've always believed that it's just as **hard** to achieve big goals as it is small ones. The only difference is that bigger goals have much more significant consequences."

⚠️ **矛盾点**：官方 25 rules 写 "as easy"，书内文和他本人多次引用是 "as hard"。两个版本意思相反但他都在用——这不是笔误，是他有意识地两面表达：**对外激励用"easy"（鼓动行动），对内决策用"hard"（提醒资源匹配）**。记录时不要调和。

### 2.4 周期观：不要抄底，让出 10-15%

- What It Takes 书中明确规则（★★★，经 Oak REI / Yahoo Finance 多处转引 ★）：
  > "It's better to give up the first 10% to 15% of a market recovery to ensure that you are buying at the right time."
  > "Most public and private investors buy too early and underestimate the severity of recessions."
- 他经历的 downturns（本人反复强调）：1973, 1975, 1982, 1987, 1990-92, 2001, 2008-10，疫情 2020 是第 8 次 ★★
  《财经》2021-04 专访确认 ★★
- **识别市场顶部的 3 个信号**（What It Takes ★★★）：
  1. Overconfidence — "this time is different"
  2. Cheap debt 助推加杠杆并购
  3. Everyone getting rich — 没有真战略的人也能赚钱

### 2.5 "Time wounds all deals"（时间是交易之敌）

- Rule #18 ★★★
- 意味：拖得越久，坏消息越多；谈判要"把所有人留在桌边直到成交"
- 这条和 2.4 的"慢慢等反弹"看似矛盾但不是：**交易执行阶段要快，市场择时阶段要慢**

## 三、自创框架：Blackstone 投资委员会决策流程（源于 Edgcomb 失败）

### 3.1 Edgcomb Steel 惨败（1989，★★★）

- 交易规模：$330M
- 触发事件：新合伙人带来"独家看项目"机会；另一位合伙人警告 Edgcomb 利润只来自钢铁库存 gain，钢价反转就完蛋；Schwarzman 没听。
- 2-3 个月后钢价见顶下跌，Edgcomb 无法付本息。
- Nyack 某 LP 把他叫去办公室骂他"one of the dumbest people he ever met"，他几乎哭出来。
- 代价：一名合伙人被迫离职。
- Schwarzman 称其为 "Blackstone 最重要的一笔交易"——因为它催生了投委会系统。

来源：
- Private Equity Insights ★
  https://pe-insights.com/how-blackstone-ceo-stephen-schwarzmans-330m-mistake-improved-the-companys-investment-process/
- King of Capital（David Carey & John Morris 著）★
- What It Takes 书中章节 "There Are No Brave, Old People in Finance" ★★★

### 3.2 决策流程 5 条硬规则（Schwarzman LinkedIn 2020-02 "What It Takes to Make Good Decisions" ★★★ + Wharton Knowledge 访谈 ★★）

1. **Bring more people to the table** — 没有"所罗门王"式独断；杜绝"单人放行"
2. **Take your time** — 备忘录至少提前 2 天发出，抵制时间压力
3. **Focus on the risk** — 讨论几乎只谈下行；**"Only criticism" 规则**：发言只能谈分析弱点和风险，不能当啦啦队
4. **Everyone must speak** — 从最资浅到最资深，全员必须发言（并且要问最接近项目的初级员工，不是只听 lead partner）
5. **Everyone owns the outcome** — 分析结束后，责任从 deal team 转移到整个 committee（**去个人化**）

**Schwarzman 原话（★★★）：**
> "If everyone is attacking you all the time, then no one is attacking you."
> "Investing is like playing basketball without a shot clock… We obsess about the downside of every potential deal until we are certain we cannot miss."
> "The best investments are the easiest ones to approve."（Business Insider 2015 ★★）

**对手错在哪（Schwarzman 批评）：**
- 把风险外包给单一 Chief Risk Officer（作为 profit center）
- 没做 distributed risk assessment
- "Don't understand how people behave"

### 3.3 迭代评估

团队经常要**回去改 2-3 轮**，书面回答所有问题，直到"没有惊喜"才过会（strategy+business 2020 ★★★）。

## 四、人才观：10s / 9s / 8s / 7s 分级

来源：
- Stanford GSB 2014-12 "You Can't Give Praise Unless It's Deserved" ★★★
  https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/ （View From The Top 演讲系列）
- Rule #23 ★★★
- Business Insider 2019-09 ★★

**原文分级（★★★）：**
- **10s**：know how to sense problems, design solutions, and do new things. "If you attract 10s, they always make it rain." 吸引并招募其他 10s
- **9s**：great at executing good strategies, but not great strategies. "A firm full of nines, that's a winning firm"
- **8s**：just do stuff that you tell them
- **7s and below**："we don't tolerate them"

**面试方法（Business Insider ★★）**：给候选人看真实的 Blackstone 场景，测 **"synchronicity in thinking"**——看他的思路能否和自己"seamlessly"同步。优先 skill set > culture fit（注意：这点和很多硅谷公司相反）。

## 五、大交易方法论：Hilton & Equity Office Properties（EOP）

### 5.1 EOP（2007，$39B，全球史上最大 LBO）

- 从 Sam Zell 手里买下
- **关键动作**：Schwarzman 警觉市场过热，让 Jonathan Gray 团队立即剥离约 $30B 物业 de-risk
- 结果：合伙人投资翻 3 倍
- 来源：Forbes Australia "Blackstone has an $80 trillion opportunity" ★
  https://www.forbes.com.au/news/investing/blackstone-80-trillion-opportunity/
- FT 原报道：https://www.ft.com/content/0ad335d0-b6c4-11db-8bc2-0000779e2340 ★★★（付费墙）

### 5.2 Hilton（2007，$26B）

- 2007-10 刚 close 就遇金融危机
- 减值 71%
- 应对：折扣价回购债务 + 再注资 $800M
- 2013 年 IPO，最终获利约 $14B——**黑石史上最大单笔盈利**
- 来源：Reuters 2007-07-03；NYT DealBook 2007-07-03；Forbes Australia 综述 ★

### 5.3 大交易的元原则（书中 + 多访谈 ★★★）

- "Pursue huge opportunities others ignore"（Rule #16）
- 危机中的资产最便宜但也最可怕——黑石用 Don't Lose Money 流程 + 充足 dry powder（2020 疫情时 $150B 现金，Yicai 2020-04 ★★）来锁定下行
- "Most fun stage of the cycle" = 别人悲观、自己有弹药的时候（2024-10 Q3 Earnings ★★）

## 十、待解矛盾与张力（不调和，直接记录）

1. **"as easy" vs "as hard"**：官方 rules 与书内文并存相反措辞（见 2.1）
2. **"Time wounds all deals" vs "不要抄底，让出 10-15%"**：交易执行要快 / 市场择时要慢——他本人没在一个地方同时讲清楚这个二分
3. **反监管 op-ed（2015）vs 危机后黑石是影子银行最大赢家**：公开立场与商业利益高度一致，不应作为"中立政策观点"引用（见 §8）
4. **Schwarzman Scholars / 深度中国敞口 vs 美国对华 AI 竞争话语**：他同时是"中美理解"最大私人捐助者 **和** MIT AI 捐款时用"对华领导权"话术（§7）——典型的"两头下注"式生存策略，不是认知失调
5. **"Hire 10s" vs "9s 组成的公司才是赢家"**：Stanford 演讲里 9s 是"winning firm"的基础，但 Rule #23 强调招 10s——他实际意思是**10s 是稀缺的锚点，9s 是规模化的主力**，不要把两句话当同一层级的原则

## 十二、给 skill 蒸馏的重点提取（TL;DR）

**真信念候选（出现 3+ 次、跨一手多源）：**
1. Don't lose money（最高优先级，决定流程设计）
2. Think big（"as hard / as easy" 二义并用）
3. Pattern recognition 靠规模 + 数据 + Monday meeting
4. 投委会法则：全员发言 + 只谈风险 + 集体担责（Edgcomb 后血的教训）
5. 周期观：不抄底，让出 10-15%；识别顶部三信号
6. Hire 10s / 9s / 8s 分级
7. Time wounds all deals（执行快）

**自创术语：**
- "10s / 9s / 8s"
- "Don't Lose Money" 流程
- "Distributed risk assessment"
- "Pattern Recognition"（品牌化为产品线）
- "It's as hard/easy to do big as small"
- "Time wounds all deals"
- Monday Meeting（跨业务线全球视频）

**推荐书单 / 智识源（调研中未发现他本人公开系统化的 reading list）**：
- 从演讲和引用痕迹看：基础训练是 Yale 本科 + HBS MBA + Lehman（Peterson 搭档）；他反复提及 Peter Peterson、André Meyer（Lazard）作为 mentor；对 Buffett / Marks 惺惺相惜（Wharton 同台）。
