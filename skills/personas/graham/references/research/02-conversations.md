# 02 · Benjamin Graham 的长对话、访谈与即兴思考

> 本文聚焦 Graham 在非著作场景（访谈、演讲、证词、课堂、学生转述）中的原话与思维方式。
> 所有引用均标注期刊、演讲名、页码或采访者，信息源排除知乎/公众号/百度百科。

---

## 一、两篇 1976 年「遗嘱级」访谈（去世前 6 个月）

### 1. "A Conversation with Benjamin Graham" — *Financial Analysts Journal*, Sep/Oct 1976

此文并非 Hartman Butler 的那次采访，而是 Charles D. Ellis 从 Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette 研讨会整理、提交给 FAJ 的书面 Q&A。两篇常被混淆，但内容互补。

**关于华尔街本质（第二问，全文最被引用的段落）**：

> "A highly unfavorable — even a cynical — one. The Stock Exchanges appear to me chiefly as a **John Bunyan type of Vanity Fair, or a Falstaffian joke, that frequently degenerates into a madhouse — 'a tale full of sound and fury, signifying nothing.'** The stock market resembles a huge laundry in which institutions take in large blocks of each other's washing — nowadays to the tune of 30 million shares a day — without true rhyme or reason. But technologically it is remarkably well-organized."
> — *FAJ* Sep/Oct 1976, "A Conversation with Benjamin Graham"

Graham 在一句话里连引三个文学典故：班扬《天路历程》的名利场、莎士比亚的 Falstaff（膨胀自大的虚言骑士）、《麦克白》第五幕「充满喧哗与骚动、毫无意义」。这是他 60 年观察华尔街后给出的盖棺之语。

**立场转变——放弃精密证券分析（本次调研的最关键段落）**：

> **Q:** "In selecting the common stock portfolio, do you advise careful study of and selectivity among different issues?"
>
> **BG:** "In general, no. **I am no longer an advocate of elaborate techniques of security analysis in order to find superior value opportunities.** This was a rewarding activity, say, 40 years ago, when our textbook 'Graham and Dodd' was first published; but the situation has changed a great deal since then. In the old days any well-trained security analyst could do a good professional job of selecting undervalued issues through detailed studies; but in the light of the enormous amount of research now being carried on, I doubt whether in most cases such extensive efforts will generate sufficiently superior selections to justify their cost. **To that very limited extent I'm on the side of the 'efficient market' school of thought now generally accepted by the professors.**"
> — 同上

注意措辞严谨性（哥大法学院＋哲学双修的痕迹）：
- 用 "in general, no" 而非 "no"——保留例外空间；
- 用 "I am no longer an advocate"（我不再是倡导者）而非 "I reject"（我拒绝）——承认历史合理性；
- 用 "to that very limited extent"（仅就此非常有限的意义上）给 EMH 让步——立刻划清边界。

**他推荐的替代方法——"simple formula approach"**：

> "I have a considerable amount of doubt on the question of how successful analysts can be overall when applying these selectivity approaches. The work I have done in the last few years has been directed to the group approach. I am trying to find out just what kind of results one would obtain from fairly simple criteria of selecting undervalued stocks — fairly simple criteria applied to not one stock but to a group of stocks."
>
> 推荐的具体公式：
> - 买入价不超过过去 12 个月盈利的 **7 倍 PE**；或
> - **股息收益率 >7%**；或
> - 股价低于净流动资产（net-net）的 2/3。
>
> 回测 1925–1975 年，年化 >15%，约为道琼斯的两倍。

这是他与 1934 年《证券分析》时代精密分析方法的**决定性切割**：不再相信普通分析师能通过深度研究战胜群体，转而信任**机械的、群体化的、可自动执行的简单规则**——这实质上是现代 factor investing 的鼻祖。

**对机构投资者的评价（同一访谈）**：

> "The thought that the DJIA could actually outperform the professionals with their army of analysts seems quite absurd. Yet that is what has been happening."

**他推荐散户读什么**：

> "I have lost most of the interest I had in the details of security analysis which I devoted myself to so strenuously for many years. I feel that they are relatively unimportant... in a sense this makes me close to the 'efficient market' theorists."

来源 PDF：*Financial Analysts Journal*, Sep/Oct 1976, pp. 20–23（grahamanddoddsville.net 存档；bylo.org/bgraham76.html 有 HTML 版）。

### 2. "An Hour with Mr. Graham" — Hartman L. Butler Jr., CFA

1976 年 3 月 6 日于加州 La Jolla Graham 家中录音采访，距其去世 6.5 个月。Butler 是 CFA 协会成员。格式为 HB/BG 对答。

**关于 GEICO（访谈开场，Buffett 后来买入 GEICO 的背景声）**：

> **HB:** "Do you think GEICO will survive?"
>
> **BG:** "Yes, I think it will survive. There is no basic reason why it won't survive, but naturally I ask myself whether the company did expand much too fast without taking into account the possibilities of these big losses. **It makes me shudder to think of the amounts of money they were able to lose in one year. Incredible!** It is surprising how many of the large companies have managed to turn in losses of $50 million or $100 million in one year, in these last few years. Something unheard of in the old days. **You have to be a genius to lose that much money.**"

**关于 1929 年崩盘中的自己（罕见的自我检讨）**：

> "All I knew was that prices were too high... **But I owed money, which was a mistake.**"

这是他承认自己违反安全边际原则的一次坦白——问题不是判断错（他知道贵），而是用了杠杆。

**对年轻分析师的建议（全文最被引用段落之一）**：

> **HB:** "Mr. Graham, what advice would you have to a young man or woman coming along now who wants to be a security analyst and a Chartered Financial Analyst?"
>
> **BG:** "I would tell them to study the past record of the stock market, study their own capabilities, and find out whether they can identify an approach to investment they feel would be satisfactory in their own case. And if they have done that, **pursue that without any reference to what other people do or think or say.** Stick to their own methods. If he or she reads *The Intelligent Investor* — **which I feel would be more useful than *Security Analysis* of the two books** — and selects from what we say some approach which one thinks would be profitable, then I say that one should do this and stick to it."

注意晚年他自己**更推荐《聪明的投资者》而不是《证券分析》**——与 1934 年以技术分析家自居的形象完全反转。

**关于简单公式的自信**：

> "I think we can do it successfully with a few techniques and simple principles. The main point is to have the right general principles and the character to stick to them."
> "**Imagine — there seems to be practically a foolproof way of getting good results out of common stock investment with a minimum of work.**"

来源：Hartman L. Butler Jr., "An Hour with Mr. Graham", March 6, 1976, La Jolla CA（grahamanddoddsville.net 存档 PDF）。

## 二、1974 年 "The Renaissance of Value" 演讲

1973–74 年熊市重创"漂亮 50"之后，Graham 作为 80 岁老兵的公开演讲。核心是**价值投资的复权**。

**关于散户相对机构的优势**：

> 散户可从约 3,000 只股票中选择，而机构被迫局限于 300 只大盘股。机构越来越像一个彼此拷贝作业的俱乐部——"taking in large blocks of each other's washing"（这个比喻 Graham 两次使用）。

**结尾给分析师的遗训**（被反复引用）：

> "Let me emphasize that it does not take a genius or even a superior talent to be successful as a value analyst. What it needs is: **first, reasonably good intelligence; second, sound principles of operation; third, and most important, firmness of character.** But whatever path you follow as financial analysts, hold on to your **moral and intellectual integrity.**"

Graham 把"性格坚定"排在"正确原则"之上——这延续了他 1934 年起的一贯立场：价值投资的难处不在智商，而在**能不能顶住孤独和群体压力**。

来源：*The Renaissance of Value*, 1974；收录于 Jason Zweig & Rodney Sullivan (eds.), *Benjamin Graham: Building a Profession*, McGraw-Hill 2010。

## 三、"投票机 vs 称重机"——原文与误传

**真正的原文（《证券分析》1934 年首版）**：

> *Security Analysis* (1934), Part 1, Ch. 1, p. 23:
> "In other words, **the market is not a weighing machine, on which the value of each issue is recorded by an exact and impersonal mechanism, in accordance with its specific qualities. Rather should we say that the market is a voting machine**, whereon countless individuals register choices which are the product partly of reason and partly of emotion."

> *Security Analysis* (1934), Part 5, Ch. 39, p. 452:
> "The stock market is a voting machine rather than a weighing machine."

**流行版本**"In the short run, the market is a voting machine but in the long run it is a weighing machine"**并非 Graham 原话**，而是 Warren Buffett 在 1973 年以后多次转述时加入"短期/长期"二分法所成。Buffett 在 1987 年、1993 年股东信中多次将其归给 Graham。Graham 原意更绝对：市场**始终**是投票机，分析师的工作是独立完成称重那一步。

## 四、Buffett 转述的 Graham（补充 Graham 本人访谈外的侧证）

- **A+ 轶事**：Buffett 是 Graham 哥大 22 年教学生涯中**唯一**拿到 A+ 的学生（1951 春季 Security Analysis 课）。来源：Alice Schroeder, *The Snowball* (2008); *Warren Buffett on Business*, O'Reilly; Columbia Business School 公开档案。
- **拒绝雇佣**：Graham 最初拒绝雇 Buffett（"We only hire Jews"——这是 Graham 本人因受华尔街反犹歧视所成的反向政策）；Buffett 曾主动提出白干，仍被拒；三年后 1954 年 Graham 才把他招进 Graham-Newman。
- **Mr. Market 寓言**：出自《聪明的投资者》第 8 章。Buffett 在 1987 Berkshire 股东信中评价："Ben Graham, my friend and teacher, long ago described the mental attitude toward market fluctuations that I believe to be most conducive to investment success. He said that you should imagine market quotations as coming from a remarkably accommodating fellow named Mr. Market..."

## 五、Graham 的"即兴类比"库

Graham 是法学+哲学双料训练者，回答从不滑入口号，总用**具体类比**把原则落地：

1. **"生意伙伴"类比**（Mr. Market, *The Intelligent Investor* 第 8 章）：把股市想象成一个躁郁症邻居，每天来报一个买卖价；他报价非理性，你没有义务接单。
2. **"拍卖行"**（*Security Analysis* 1934, p. 23）：市场是**投票机**不是**称重机**，登记的是"部分理性、部分情绪"的选择。
3. **"洗衣房"**（1974, 1976 反复用）：机构彼此拿对方的脏衣服来洗——30 million shares/day "without true rhyme or reason"。
4. **"Vanity Fair / Falstaffian joke / madhouse"**（1976 FAJ）：文学递进式三连引。
5. **"称重"的对应动作**：价值分析师的工作不是预测市场，而是独立完成"称重"那一步——**安全边际**就是给自己的秤留刻度误差。

## 六、Graham 回避或拒绝回答的问题类型

通读 1976 两篇访谈可以归纳出 Graham 的回避模式：

- **拒绝预测短期市场方向**："trying, valiantly and ineffectively, to do things they can't do well" —— 他把短期预测归为分析师群体的病。
- **拒绝点评具体股票的买卖时机**：谈 GEICO 时他只谈结构性问题（扩张过快、巨额亏损），不给买卖建议。
- **拒绝承认自己有"天赋"**："It does not take a genius..."——他坚持把价值投资归功于**方法**与**性格**，而非智商。
- **回避对凯恩斯的直接比较**：虽然两人同代且都投资，但 Graham 在访谈中极少评价凯恩斯的投机/投资二分法；间接痕迹在《聪明的投资者》第 1 章定义 investment vs speculation 时，明显修订了凯恩斯的边界。
- **对 Fisher-style "growth at any price"**：Graham 不排斥成长股，但坚持"**must justify every purchase... by impersonal, objective reasoning... a margin of safety, in value terms**"（1976 FAJ）——任何价格下的成长故事都必须通过安全边际检验。

## 七、关于"风险管理 vs 收益管理"那句引文的溯源

广为流传的 "The essence of investment management is the management of risks, not the management of returns" 在 AZQuotes 等二手库中被归给 Graham 1934 年的《证券分析》，但**未能找到确切页码**，可能是现代改写。

**可验证的等价原文**来自 1974 年 Financial Analysts Research Foundation 研讨会发言：

> "Real investment risk is measured not by the percent that a stock may decline in price... but by the danger of a loss of quality and earnings power through economic changes or deterioration in management."

以及 *The Intelligent Investor* 第 20 章 "Margin of Safety" 的总论：安全边际是"**the central concept of investment**"——风险的控制优先于收益的追求。

## 八、立场演化总结（1934 → 1976）

| 维度 | 1934《证券分析》时代 | 1976 最后访谈时代 |
|------|---------------------|------------------|
| 分析方法 | 精密、逐股深度研究 | 简单公式、群体化应用 |
| 市场有效性 | 强调市场非理性可利用 | 承认专业分析师整体难战胜指数（"to that very limited extent"） |
| 推荐读物 | 《证券分析》 | 《聪明的投资者》 |
| 核心工具 | 财报三表深挖、质量评级 | 7×PE / 7% 股息 / 净流动资产 2/3 三条简单筛子 |
| 对散户建议 | 学会分析 | 别学分析了，用规则 |
| 不变的内核 | 安全边际、性格、独立思考 | 安全边际、性格、独立思考 |

**唯一不变的三件事**：margin of safety、firmness of character、intellectual integrity。
