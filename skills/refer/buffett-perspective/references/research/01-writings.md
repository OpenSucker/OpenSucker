# 01 — Warren Buffett 著作与系统性长文调研

**调研范围**：Berkshire 股东信(1965–2023)、Partnership Letters(1957–1969)、1984 Columbia 演讲、1998 Florida 演讲、2008 NYT op-ed、推荐书目、承认错误的文本。
**聚焦**：投资决策。

---

## 一、核心自创术语(≥3 次反复出现)

**1. Economic Moat(经济护城河)** — 1995/1999/2005/2007 letters 反复。2007 原文："A truly great business must have an enduring 'moat' that protects excellent returns on invested capital." 城堡比喻：护城河越宽越深越多鳄鱼越好。标志包括低成本(GEICO/Costco)、品牌(See's/KO/Apple)、网络效应(AmEx)、规模经济。[Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/economic-moat-why-warren-buffett-160046125.html)

**2. Owner Earnings(所有者盈余)** — **1986 letter 附录(Scott Fetzer 讨论)**首次提出。公式：报告利润 + 折旧摊销非现金项 − 维护性资本支出(注意是 maintenance CapEx 而非总 CapEx；要求判断而非精确)。Buffett 原话："honest approximations rather than precisely calculated figures." 这是他 DCF 里折现的对象，不是 GAAP 净利润也不是 FCF。[Seven Corners](https://www.sevencornerscapital.com/single-post/2017/01/18/berkshire-1986-shareholder-letter-cliffs-notes-version) | [Old School Value](https://www.oldschoolvalue.com/stock-valuation/what-is-owner-earnings/)

**3. Intrinsic Value(内在价值)** — **1992 letter** 明确定义并归功 John Burr Williams(1938)："the discounted value of the cash that can be taken out of a business during its remaining life." 补充："The investment shown by the discounted-flows-of-cash calculation to be the cheapest is the one that the investor should purchase — irrespective of whether the business grows or doesn't..." 特点：估计值，非精确值；必须配合 margin of safety。1994/1996/2000/2008/2014 letters 反复。[CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2018/12/05/warren-buffett-on-the-biggest-puzzle-for-investors-intrinsic-value.html)

**4. Margin of Safety(安全边际)** — 继承自 Graham。1992 letter："We insist on a margin of safety in our purchase price. If we calculate the value of a common stock to be only slightly higher than its price, we're not interested in buying." Graham 的桥梁类比(Buffett 多次转述)："建桥设计承重 30000 磅，但只让 10000 磅的卡车过——投资也一样。" 2007 股东会补充：安全边际大小取决于生意可预测性。[CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2024/03/10/heres-what-warren-buffett-means-by-investing-with-a-margin-of-safety.html)

**5. Circle of Competence(能力圈)** — **1996 letter**："You only have to be able to evaluate companies within your circle of competence. The size of that circle is not very important; knowing its boundaries, however, is vital." 另一句："If we have a strength, it is in recognizing when we are operating well within our circle of competence and when we are approaching the perimeter." 1993/1996/1999/2000 letters 反复。注意："know what you don't know" 更常归 Munger。[Investment Masters](http://mastersinvest.com/circleofconfidencequotes)

**6. Mr. Market(市场先生)** — 源自 Graham，**1987 letter** 做了最经典引申："Under these conditions, the more manic-depressive his behavior, the better for you." Cinderella-at-the-ball 警告：沉迷 Mr. Market 报价越深，舞会越可能待到南瓜时刻。核心立场：Mr. Market 来服务你，不是指导你。1987/1997/2017 letters 反复。[GuruFocus](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/1974508/warren-buffetts-early-letters-1987)

**7. Float(保险浮存金)** — 1967 年起股东信保险章节几乎每年出现。2011 letter："This business produces 'float' – money that doesn't belong to us, but that we get to invest for Berkshire's benefit." **Cost of float 概念**：承保盈利时，float 成本为负——Berkshire 被付钱来持有可投资资金。这是 Berkshire 长期复利的核心：负成本杠杆 × 股权复利。GEICO 低成本直销模式是高质量 float 来源。据报 2022 年 float 从 $147B 增至 $164B。[NPR Planet Money](https://www.npr.org/sections/money/2010/03/warren_buffett_explains_the_ge.html) | [Insurance Journal](https://www.insurancejournal.com/news/national/2012/02/27/237253.htm)

**8. Look-Through Earnings(穿透盈余)** — **1990 letter** 首次系统阐述。公式：报告营业利润 + Berkshire 在主要被投资公司未按 GAAP 反映的留存盈余份额 − 假设分配这些留存盈余的税。1990 letter："look-through earnings must increase at about 15% annually if our intrinsic business value is to grow at that rate." 背景：KO 大量保留利润不分红，GAAP 只记分红，但经济实质是为 Berkshire 创造价值。[The Investments Blog](http://theinvestmentsblog.blogspot.com/2011/07/buffett-on-look-through-earnings.html)

**9. "Wonderful business at a fair price vs. fair business at a wonderful price"** — **1989 letter** 公开承认从 Graham 式烟蒂股转向优质企业，明确归功 Munger。原文："It's far better to buy a wonderful company at a fair price than a fair company at a wonderful price." 同 letter 自我批评烟蒂股："A cigar butt found on the street that has only one puff left in it may not offer much of a smoke, but the 'bargain purchase' will make that puff all profit." Buffett 承认这在 Berkshire 规模大了之后不可行，且常陷价值陷阱。[Acquirer's Multiple](https://acquirersmultiple.com/2024/08/warren-buffett-why-i-transitioned-from-cigar-butts-to-quality-companies/)

**10. Opportunity Cost(机会成本)** — 多次股东会原话："Charlie and I view our cost of capital as what can be produced by our second best idea." 不使用 CAPM 或 WACC；每个买入决策与下一个最好备选比较。这也解释了为什么常年保有巨额现金。相关：Buffett 拒绝 Beta/波动率作为风险度量——他的风险定义是"**永久性资本损失的概率**"。[Value Investing World](https://www.valueinvestingworld.com/2014/05/charlie-munger-on-opportunity-costs.html)

## 二、反复系统性论点

**11. "Price is what you pay; value is what you get."** 2008 letter 归 Graham："Long ago, Ben Graham taught me that..." 1998 Florida 演讲 / 2008 letter / 多次股东会反复。

**12. "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful."** 1986 letter 首次出现，**2008 年 10 月 16 日 NYT op-ed "Buy American. I Am."** 最著名——Buffett 在危机底部宣布私人账户买美股。[MaxDividends](https://www.maxdividends.com/p/sunday-coffee-warren-buffett-oct-43e)

**13. 衍生品是"金融大规模杀伤性武器"** — **2002 letter**："We view [derivatives] as time bombs... financial weapons of mass destruction..." 2008 验证。矛盾点：Berkshire 自身也在卖长期股指 put。[ShareOK 学术](https://shareok.org/entities/undpublication/adf91992-68e8-4003-8123-c4e35de636c2)

**14. 风险 ≠ 波动率；风险 = 永久性资本损失。** 1993 letter 明确对抗 beta/CAPM，1984 Columbia 演讲与多次股东会复述。Buffett 与学术金融最持久的对立立场。

**15. 打孔卡比喻(Punch Card)** — 一生只能打 20 次孔。1998 Florida 演讲最完整；多次股东会与《Poor Charlie's Almanack》转述。[Saber Capital](https://sabercapitalmgt.com/warren-buffett-1998-talk-at-university-of-florida/)

**16. 集中持股反对过度分散** — 1993 letter："Diversification is protection against ignorance. It makes little sense if you know what you are doing." 但晚期现金过剩与此有张力(保留不调和)。

**17. 不碰科技(直到 Apple)** — 1999–2001 letters 明确承认避开互联网但不后悔(超出能力圈)。2016 买入 Apple 的 reframe：**Apple 不是科技公司而是消费品公司**——品牌忠诚、定价权、现金流。2023 底 Apple 约占 Berkshire 市值 22%、股权组合 20%。[Forbes 2016](https://www.forbes.com/sites/gurufocus/2016/05/23/warren-buffetts-billion-dollar-apple-investment/) | [Carnegie Investment](https://blog.carnegieinvest.com/warren-buffett-and-the-evolution-of-berkshire-hathaways-apple-investment)

**18. 等待好球(Ted Williams 打击区原理)** — 1997/2005/2014 letters 引用《The Science of Hitting》。当代印证：2024 Berkshire 现金 $334B(2023 $167.6B)，2025 据报超 $370B。[AOL/Yahoo](https://www.aol.com/finance/only-swing-pitches-warren-buffett-110100744.html)

**19. "Our favorite holding period is forever"** — **1988 letter** 关于 KO。1988/1996/2006 letters 反复。矛盾：多次打破(IBM、航空、大量银行股)——这是倾向而非教条。

**20. 管理层诚信 + 能力是前提** — 1993/2000/2002 letters 反复："Hire well-intentioned and able people, and treat them as partners." 与骗子或无能者打交道再便宜也不做。

## 三、立场演化(最重要的迁移)

**阶段 1｜1957–1969 合伙企业时期，纯 Graham 烟蒂股** — Sanborn Map(地图公司但持有庞大可变现证券组合)、Dempster Mill(农机厂，派 Harry Bottle 做激进 turnaround)、**Berkshire Hathaway 本身**(纺织厂——Buffett 自称最糟糕投资)、American Express(1963 salad oil 丑闻中逆势买入)。方法：净净股、资产重估、激进重组。[ValuePickr PDF](https://forum.valuepickr.com/uploads/short-url/7QsaAqDYEUJCAPpPfBOg7zWNy8V.pdf) | [Investors Podcast](https://www.theinvestorspodcast.com/articles/cigar-butt-investing/) | [GuruFocus](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/259207/four-timeless-lessons-from-young-buffett)

**阶段 2｜1972 See's Candies——转折点** — $25M 收购付 3× book value，Graham 式标准下"太贵"。Munger 强推：按这价出，不然走人。实际结果 50 多年累积利润 $2B+，投入极少。Buffett 领悟品牌定价权。Munger 原话："At Berkshire, Warren and I raised the prices of See's Candy a little faster than others might have." [Saber Capital](https://sabercapitalmgt.com/buffett-1972-letter-to-sees-candies/) | [25iq](https://25iq.com/2016/11/25/a-dozen-things-warren-buffett-and-charlie-munger-learned-from-sees-candies/)

**阶段 3｜1988 KO + 1989 letter 范式正式切换** — 1989 letter 首次写下"wonderful business at fair price"原则，KO 成教科书案例。

**阶段 4｜2016–2024 Apple + 超常现金储备** — 能力圈扩张；但现金史无前例——规模倒逼哲学迁移，与早期"集中"立场有张力(不调和)。

## 四、自己承认的错误

**Dexter Shoe(1993)——"最惨烈错误"** — 2007/2014 letters 反复提起。2014 letter："What I had assessed as durable competitive advantage vanished within a few years." 毁灭性细节：用 Berkshire **股票**支付，Dexter 归零同时那批股票升值数十倍，估算总损失 $3.5B–$9B。之后几乎不再用股票收购。[Business Insider](https://markets.businessinsider.com/news/stocks/warren-buffett-most-gruesome-mistake-dexter-shoe-9-billion-error-2020-1-1028827359)

**IBM(2011–2018)** — 2011 买入 2017–2018 清仓，基本打平或小亏。2018 CNBC 承认误判 IBM 云计算地位。讽刺：清仓 IBM 同时加仓 Apple——他承认选错科技股标的，而非科技股本身。

**航空股(2016 买 4 家，2020 全部卖出)** — 2020 年 5 月 2 日虚拟年度股东会原话："It turned out I was wrong. ... The airline business changed in a very major way." 据报另有："Our airline position was a mistake. Berkshire is worth less today because I took that [position]." 讽刺：1989–1996 letters 几乎每年把 US Airways 当错误典型，2016 仍再次入场——纪律在 30 年破坏两次。且卖出接近底部，错误叠加(买错+卖错)。[Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/sergeiklebnikov/2020/05/02/warren-buffett-sells-airline-stocks-amid-coronavirus-i-made-a-mistake/) | [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2020/05/02/warren-buffett-says-berkshire-sold-its-entire-position-in-airlines-because-of-the-coronavirus.html) | [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-52518186)

**Kraft Heinz(2015)——付贵了** — 承认**不是**在 2017 letter，而是 **2019 年 2 月 CNBC 访谈**(在 Kraft Heinz 巨额减值之后)。原话："I was wrong in a couple of ways on Kraft Heinz. We overpaid for Kraft." 归因：Costco/Walmart 侵蚀老消费品牌对零售商的议价能力。[FT](https://www.ft.com/content/c6d7dd12-38f8-11e9-b72b-2c7f526ca5d0) | [WSJ](https://www.wsj.com/articles/warren-buffett-i-overpaid-for-kraft-heinz-11551098035) | [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/article/business/warren-buffett-says-berkshire-overpaid-for-kraft-heinz-idUSKCN1QE1EV/)

**Berkshire Hathaway 纺织厂本体——"最愚蠢投资"** — 因前任 CEO 砍 1/8 美元报价赌气接手濒死纺织厂，估算此情绪决定 20 年里损失 $200B 潜在复利。教训：情绪驱动 + 沉没成本陷阱。

## 五、1984 Columbia "Superinvestors of Graham-and-Doddsville" 演讲(一手)

*Hermes* 1984 秋。核心：(1)**掷硬币比喻**——2.25 亿美国人掷硬币会有 215 人连掷 20 次正面，但**如果全来自同一个小村庄呢**？(2)证据：9 位长期大幅跑赢 S&P 的投资人(Schloss/Tweedy-Browne/Sequoia-Ruane/Munger/Pacific-Guerin/Perlmeter/Washington Post & FMC 养老金)，持仓几乎不重叠——证明不是抄作业。(3)共同框架：寻找价格-价值折扣(margin of safety)。(4)反 EMH。(5)元反转：EMH 越被学界接受，反而给 value investor 留下更多机会。[Acquirer's Multiple Fresh Transcript](https://acquirersmultiple.com/2016/08/the-superinvestors-of-graham-and-doddsville-freshly-transcribed-2016-part-1/) | [Kenyan Wall Street](https://kenyanwallstreet.com/buffetts-speech-at-columbia-business-school-in-1984-the-superinvestors-of-graham-doddsville)

## 六、1998 University of Florida 演讲(一手)

dot-com 泡沫前夕对 MBA 的长讲座。警告互联网公司无法估值(超能力圈)；强调 price vs value；**打孔卡比喻**最完整版本出现在此；两年后 dot-com 崩盘验证。[Saber Capital 笔记](https://sabercapitalmgt.com/warren-buffett-1998-talk-at-university-of-florida/) | [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0K7lZ58ajxA)

## 八、内在矛盾(保留不调和)

1. **集中 vs 现金过剩**：1993 letter 反分散 vs 晚期 $300B+ 现金
2. **永远持有 vs 清仓**：IBM/航空/银行股
3. **不要择时 vs 本人择时**：2008 op-ed 本身即一次择时声明；2020 卖航空是失败择时
4. **反衍生品 vs 自用衍生品**：2002 letter vs 卖股指 put
5. **安全边际教条 vs 优质生意可不打折**：1992 letter vs 2007 股东会
6. **能力圈**：坚决不投科技(1999–2015)→ Apple 占比一度超 40%
7. **烟蒂股 vs 优质生意**：两种几乎相反方法论共存过

这些矛盾**就是真实决策过程**——Buffett 是被现实与规模不断修改的实用主义者，非教条主义者。
